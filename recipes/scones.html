Scones

Description

Gluten free scones are surprisingly easy to make at home, and very quick to bake. 
          Even inexperienced bakers should do well with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 50g (12.4oz) gluten free self-raising flour
  • sp gluten free baking powder
  • tbsp caster sugar
  • 0.5 tsp xanthan gum
  • 0.5 tsp fine salt
  • 100g (3.5oz) unsalted butter
  • 20ml (4 fl oz) semi-skimmed milk
  • medium eggs (plus 1 egg for glazing)
  • Optional: 100g (3.5oz) raisins
